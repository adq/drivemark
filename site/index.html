DriveMark

Save and sync your bookmarks across devices — stored in a Google Sheet in your own Google Drive.

Add to Chrome Privacy Policy

How it works

  1. Sign in with your Google account.
  2. Pick (or create) a Google Sheet to hold your bookmarks.
  3. Save any page — it's written straight to that Sheet, and every device you sign in on stays in sync.

Features

  • 📄 Your bookmarks live in a normal Google Sheet you own and can open, edit, or export any time.
  • 🔄 Cross-device sync via Google Drive — no separate account, no server in the middle.
  • 🗂️ Organise bookmarks into folders, with notes, excerpts, and cover images.
  • 🔒 Minimal access: DriveMark can only touch the one spreadsheet it creates or that you open with it.

Your data stays yours

DriveMark uses the narrow drive.file Google permission, which grants access only to files the app itself creates or that you explicitly open with it — not your whole Drive. Your bookmarks are stored in your own Google Sheet. There are no DriveMark servers: nothing you save is sent to us. See the Privacy Policy for details.
